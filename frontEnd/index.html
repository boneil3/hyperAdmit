Our Mission

We help families get an acceptance letter from their dream school.

Applying to college is a mess. Prior to HyperAdmit, families who were getting started with the college admisisons process needed to digest conflicting advice from friends, family, teachers, admissions counselors, and private admissions consultants. The advice was ambiguous at best and damaging at worst. By skipping the admissions advice middlemen, we knew we could make the process easier for families HyperAdmit was born. By anonymizing and verifying you and the admissions officer, HyperAdmit lets you get best practices and position your application with a real college admissions officer.

When it comes to college admissions, only the admissions officers themselves know the secret formula. We knew we could create an easy and ethical way for admissions officers to consult families on how to best position themselves for top colleges. HyperAdmit was born.

General

How is Central Park priced?
Our pricing is straightforward. There are two options to chose from: pay-as-you-go and the package. The pay-as-you-go option is $249/hr and gives the student 20 free minutes of in-app on-demand test prep whenever needed. The package option gives you 20 hours of lessons for $3,950 - a 20% discount to our normal rate.
What type of service does my son or daughter need?
Pay-as-you-go or the package? SAT or ACT? It all depends on your goals and your son or daughter's baseline. The complementary tutoring session lets us pin down the right option.
How does your price compare to other tutoring services?
Our pricing is very competitive with other premium tutoring services in New York that offer none of the tech services that come bundled with our tutoring. Normally, tutoring companies will quote you a set rate for a fixed number of lessons over the course of a several months, and what happens inside that time is a black box. In other words, a tutor and your child may give you their opinion on how they are doing, but what actually happens on test day is anyone's guess. We do our best remove the chance from the equation by having a student complete his practice questions through our mobile applications and overlaying his progress over thousands of data points of back data.
Are tutoring sessions locked into a set schedule?
Not at all! You can adjust your scheduled sessions at any time. Students can even order on-demand lessons, video chat, or call us at their convenience!
What is the average SAT score increase for your customers?
After 10 sessions, we have found the average score increase to be just over 270 points, with the biggest increase happening in math.
I was referred by a current or past customer. Do I get a discount?
You do! Both of you and your referrer get 20% off.
